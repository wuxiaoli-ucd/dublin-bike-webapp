# Persona 1 (Xiaoli)

**Persona:** Bryan 
**Age:** 55
**Occupation:** Pilot
**Location:** From US

**Role / Context:**
US tourist in Dublin for three days and wants to explore the city with flexibility.

**Goals & Motivations:**
Ease of use for first time user / short trip
Get from A to B using the map - maybe wants the option to see landmarks 

**Needs & Pain Points:**
Gets frustrated when an app is unintuitive to use - needs there to be a simple path from home screen to the point of getting on a bike

**Behaviors & Attitudes:**
Will use whichever bike-renting app is easiest
Doesn’t care as much about money as ease of use

**Quote:**
“If I can’t open the app and be on a bike in less than 5 minutes, then the service isn’t worth my time.”



# Persona 2 (Conor)

**Persona:** Hannah 
**Age:** 25
**Occupation:** Accountant
**Location:** Dublin

**Role / Context:**
Works in Dublin and cycles to work everyday. Has never used Dublin Bikes but when her bike breaks down one day, she downloads the app. 

**Goals & Motivations:**
Finding a bike quickly as she is delayed after finding her bike doesn’t work

**Needs & Pain Points:**
The setup process must be quick, login screens can’t take up too much time

**Behaviors & Attitudes:**
Wants to try Dublin Bikes as she has seen other cyclists using them and is willing to give them a chance.

**Quote:**
“I need the app to get where I’m going for one day and if I like the service, I will use it again.”



# Persona 3 (Andrew)

**Persona:** Niamh
**Age:** 37
**Occupation:** Bartender 
**Location:** Dublin

**Role / Context:**
Uses Dublin Bikes to get from Connolly train station to her work on Drury Street.

**Goals & Motivations:**
Wants to be able to book a bike at Connolly so she can get to work on time?

**Needs & Pain Points:**
Would appreciate benefits for long-term users such as discounts/ a free ride for every 10 rides.
Frustrated when bikes are unavailable during peak hours
Be able to quickly check the weather to decide whether cycling is feasible.

**Behaviors & Attitudes:**
Happily uses Dublin Bikes as she doesn’t have to wait around for buses

**Quote:**
“I get a train from North Co. Dublin and use Dublin Bikes so I don’t have to sit on a bus after spending 40 minutes on a train.
